Detailed Description

The RoomPanel forms the main part of the MasterPresenter.

It's where the characters walk around, where the world is shown: where the Room is shown. You won't find a reference to the Room class here however. The RoomPresenter holds the Room object and updates the RoomPanel occasionally.
