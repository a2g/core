Detailed Description

The InventoryPanel is the grid of images on the bottom right of the screen.

If you click on these, the name of the object will appear in the CommandLinePanel - if it is active. You won't find a reference to the Inventory in the InventoryPanel class however: the InventoryPresenter holds the Inventory object, and updates the InventoryPanel occasionally.
