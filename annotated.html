VerbIts just two pieces of text: displayName and sentenceName; sentenceName is a sentence template for how to use the verb with both one and two objects. For example: the displayName "Walk" has a sentence name of "Walk AAA" For example: the displayName "Use" has a sentence name of "Use AAA|Use AAA with BBB" For the single object case, the "AAA" is replaced with the objects, and the term before the "|" is used. For the two object case, the "AAA" and "BBB" are replaced, and the term after the "|" is used
