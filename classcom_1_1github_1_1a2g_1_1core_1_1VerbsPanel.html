Detailed Description

The VerbsPanel is the grid of textual words that appear on the bottom left of the screen.

If you click on these the selected verb will appear in the CommoandLinePanel, if it is active. You won't find a reference to the Verbs class here however. The VerbsPresenter holds the Verbs object and updates the VerbsPanel occasionally.
